Arsenal

History:

Arsenal was founded in 1886 as Dial Square, later renamed Royal Arsenal, and finally became Arsenal in 1913.
The club moved to Highbury the same year and has since become one of England's most storied teams.
Under Herbert Chapman in the 1930s, Arsenal became a dominant force, innovating tactics and winning multiple titles.

,

Achievements:

  1. League Titles: 13 (most recent in 2004 with the "Invincibles" team, which went unbeaten).
  2. FA Cups: 14 (the most of any club, with the latest in 2020).
  3. European Success: Winners of the UEFA Cup Winners’ Cup (1994).

Legends of Arsenal

Thierry Henry: Arsenal’s all-time leading scorer and a global football icon.
Dennis Bergkamp: Known for his skill and creativity, Bergkamp defined Arsenal's golden era.
Patrick Vieira: A commanding midfielder and captain during their "Invincibles" season..
